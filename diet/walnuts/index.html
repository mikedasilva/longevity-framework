Walnuts

Why

  • Studies showing positive results with colon cancer prevention based on walnut consumption.
  • Walnuts contain the essential fatty acids omega-3 and 6
  • Reduces free radicals (aka. an antioxidant)

How to consume

  • Eat them in their raw form.
  • Crumble them up as toppers to pancakes/waffles, avocado toast

The Research

  • Colon Cancer Prevention with Walnuts: A Longitudinal Study in Mice from the Perspective of a Gut Enterotype-like Cluster
  • Effects of Walnut Consumption on Colon Carcinogenesis and Microbial Community Structure
  • A Walnut-Enriched Diet Affects Gut Microbiome in Healthy Caucasian Subjects: A Randomized, Controlled Trial
  • Changes in the gut microbial communities following addition of walnuts to the diet
  • Nut Consumption and Survival in Patients With Stage III Colon Cancer: Results From CALGB 89803 (Alliance)
