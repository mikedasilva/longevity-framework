Sugar

Why to avoid

  • Sugars are rapidly absorbed into the bloodstream, causing hyperglycemia (high blood glucose). Regular consumption of high glycemic foods may lead to insulin resistance, type 2 diabetes, and obesity.

How to spot it

  • Food labels may list various types of sugars with names such as: sugar, fructose, cane sugar, corn syrup, and about 60 other names! SugarScience has a pretty comprehensive list with more information.
  • Processed foods often have lots of sugars added.
  • Juice derived from fruits seem healthy but are actually just full of sugar. For juices, a quick rule of thumb is opt for veggie based ones and avoid fruit based. If it's gotta have fruit, smoothies that use fruit pieces blended rather than just the juice are the way to go.

How to avoid it

  • Read food labels and avoid foods with added sugars
  • Drink coffee black
  • Opt for water over juice or pop
