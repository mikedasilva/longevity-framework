Inhalation

What you inhale passes through your lungs and can end up in your blood. Air quality is of high importance when looking at minimizing breathing in harmful toxins. To help improve the quality of air around you, consider the following: use an air purifier in your bedroom get fresh air into your space use hepa filters when vacuuming and on air circulation systems (ex: purifiers, central cooling/heating) use appropriate masks when present around harmful particles (ex: dust, debris, sprays, fumes, vapors)
