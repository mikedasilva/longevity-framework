Inhalation

What you inhale passes through your lungs and can enter the bloodstream. Air quality has an outsized impact on long-term health.

What I do

  • No smoking
  • Air purifier with HEPA filter in the bedroom
  • Get fresh air into living spaces regularly
  • Use HEPA filters in vacuums and on air circulation systems
  • Wear appropriate masks when around harmful particles (dust, debris, sprays, fumes, vapors)
